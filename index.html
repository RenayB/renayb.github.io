About Me

I am a senior Stephen F. Austin State University student pursing a Bachelors in computer science. While exploring the different careers in computer science, I developed a strong interest in designing and developing software. Having the ability to develop software is great to me because I would have the opportunity to create software that could impact millions of lives in a positive way. Growing up, I’ve witnessed technology become a huge part of a person’s everyday life. Now I want to be a part of the growth in technology by developing software that can benefit the world.

Currently, I am enrolled in classes that focus on the basics of software engineering. Learning about the different life cycles and processes that software goes through has given me a great understanding about the subject. After learning the basics, I took matters into my own hands by starting a few projects of my own. I started off with this website as my first project because I wanted to explore the fundamentals of web based software. Other projects I started on were mobile based. Mobile applications have become very popular over the past few years. For this reason, I dove right into learning the fundamentals of mobile software development.

For a half of my life I resided in Los Angeles, California. Later, my family and I bought a plan ticket to Houston, Texas. We haven’t left ever since! I am grateful for being able to experience two great cities and culture. When I am not behind a computer, I am usually hanging out with friends, reading, working out , or watching sports. My favorite sports team are the Lakers and my favorite athlete is Kobe Bryant.
